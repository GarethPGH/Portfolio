About the Artist

I am a part-time professional freelance artist with 10+ years experience in various types of media.

Scratchboard is my favorite medium, but I also work professionally with watercolors, acrylic paints, different shades of pencil graphite, artist markers, colored pencils, and dabble in pastels, or charcoal on personal projects.

I produce digital images using Gimp, but have experience using Corel, Blender, various Adobe, and Microsoft products, as well as CAD

I am most experienced producing art involving animals, or fantasy creations, but I have done works involving city-scapes, backgrounds, people, and professional logos as well. My works range from small 3'' x 5'' pieces, all the way to large murals, and sculptural pieces

Need something specific? Don't hesitate to ask!
